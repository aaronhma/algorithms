# Important Things in Mathematics

## Numbers

-   $1001 = 7 * 11 * 13$
-   $2002 = 2 * 1001 = 2 * 7 * 11 * 13$
-   $111 = 3 * 37$
-   $2^{15} = 32768$
-   $2^{16} = 65536$
-   $2^{22} = 4194304$
-   $\pi = 3.1415926 \approx \frac{22}{7}$
-   $5^5 = 3125$
-   $12^2 = 144$
-   $2 * 12^2 = 24 * 12 = 288$
-   $37 * 2 = 74$
-   $37 * 3 = 111$
-   $37 * 5 = 185$
-   $\frac{1}{6} = 0.166666666... \approx 0.17$

## Conversions

-   $1 \ km = 1000 \ m$
-   $1 \ mile = 5280 \ ft$
-   $1 \ hour = 60 \ minutes$

## Words and Meanings

-   Prefix "milli" means $\frac{1}{1000}$-th
-   Prefix "kilo" means $1000$

## Important Things to Know

-   $91$ is not prime
