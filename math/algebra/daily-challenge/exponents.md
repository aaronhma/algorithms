# Exponent Rules

-   Anything to the power of $0$ equals to $1$:

$$y^0 = 1$$

-   Product rules:

$$y^m \times y^n = y^{m + n}$$
$$y^m \times z^m = (yz)^m$$

-   Quotient rules:

$$y^m \div y^n = y^{m - n}$$
$$y^m \div z^m = (y \div z)^m$$

-   Power rules:

$$(y^a)^b = y^{ab}$$
$$y^{a^b} = y^{(a^b)}$$
$$y^{\frac{m}{n}} = (\sqrt[n]{y})^m$$

-   Negative exponents:

$$y^{-n} = \frac{1}{y^n}$$
