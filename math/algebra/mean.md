## Geometric Mean

The Geometric Mean of $a$ and $b$ is defined as $\sqrt{ab}$. For example, for $4$ and $9$, the Geometric Mean is $\sqrt{4 * 9} = \sqrt{36} = 6$. Notice how $\frac{3}{2} * 4 = 6$ and $\frac{3}{2} * 6 = 9$.
